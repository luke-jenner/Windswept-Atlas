Lawful Good Outer Plane on the [[World Lore/Cosmological Wheel\|Cosmological Wheel]]. 
Host of many Lawful Good [[World Lore/Gods/Gods\|Gods]], including Pelor, Bahamut, Kord, and Moradin.
Also known as the 'Seven Heavens' - Divided into seven layers ascending a mountain.
1. Lunia - The Silver Heaven - An eternal summer's night
2. Mercuria - The Golden Heaven - A perpetual golden hour, where angels train for duty
3. Venya - The Pearly Haven - A shifting space of green and storm, home to Kord the Stormlord and The Greenfields
4. Solania - The Crystal Heaven - Home to Moradin and the Soul Forge in Erackinor
5. Mertion - The Platinum Heaven - The Dragon Aerie, home to Bahamut and his Palace
6. Jovar - The Glittering Heaven
7. Chronias - The Illuminated Heaven

Connected to [[Eos/The Cosmological Wheel/The Underground\|The Underground]] via a train station underneath Lunia
